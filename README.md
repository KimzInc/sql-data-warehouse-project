# Data Warehouse & Analytics Project

## Overview

Welcome to the **Data Warehouse & Analytics Project** repository.

This project showcases the end-to-end development of a modern data warehousing and business analytics solution. It demonstrates how raw data from multiple business systems can be transformed into a centralized, analytics-ready platform that supports reporting, business intelligence, and data-driven decision-making.

The project follows industry-standard data engineering and analytics practices, making it an excellent portfolio piece for demonstrating skills in data integration, data modeling, SQL development, and business intelligence.

---

## Project Goals

### 1. Data Warehouse Development

#### Objective

Design and implement a scalable data warehouse using **Python** and **PostgreSQL** that consolidates data from multiple operational systems into a single source of truth for analytical reporting.

#### Key Requirements

* **Data Sources**

  * Import data from two business systems:

    * Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
    * Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
  * Source data is provided in CSV format.

* **Data Quality Management**

  * Identify and resolve missing, inconsistent, and duplicate data.
  * Apply data cleansing and validation procedures before loading data into the warehouse.

* **Data Integration**

  * Merge ERP and CRM datasets into a unified analytical model.
  * Create a structure optimized for reporting, dashboarding, and business analysis.

* **Project Scope**

  * Focus exclusively on the most recent available data.
  * Historical tracking and slowly changing dimensions are outside the scope of this project.

* **Documentation**

  * Provide clear documentation of the data architecture, schema design, and business entities.
  * Ensure accessibility for both technical teams and business stakeholders.

### 2. Business Intelligence & Analytics

#### Objective

Develop SQL-based analytical solutions that generate actionable business insights and support strategic decision-making.

#### Analytics Areas

##### Customer Analytics

##### Product Analytics

* Product performance evaluation
* Best-selling product identification
* Revenue contribution analysis
* Product category insights

##### Sales Analytics

* Sales trend analysis
* Revenue growth monitoring
* Performance tracking across time periods
